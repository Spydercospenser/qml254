ICT Basics Introduction

What is ICT Trading?

Inner Circle Trader (ICT) methodologies focus on identifying how institutional traders move markets and how retail traders can align with these movements.

Core Concepts:

  • Market Structure: Identifying trends and potential reversals
  • Order Blocks: Areas where institutions place their orders
  • Fair Value Gaps: Imbalances in price that often get filled
  • Liquidity Pools: Areas where stops are likely clustered
